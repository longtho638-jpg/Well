## 1. The Dashboard: Your Mission Control

When you log in, you land on the **Dashboard**. This is designed to show you *only* what matters for your growth.

### 🏆 Road to Founder Club (The Big Goal)
At the top, you will see a large progress bar.
*   **Your Goal:** Reach **100,000,000 VNĐ** in Team Volume within 30 days.
*   **The Reward:** Unlock the **"Founder Club" Badge** + **2% Global Bonus**.
*   **Action:** Use the "Share Link" button here to invite others to join your team.

### 🤖 The Coach (Your AI Mentor)
Look for the **"The Coach"** widget in the Sidebar (Desktop) or Menu (Mobile).
*   **Daily Quests:** Simple tasks (e.g., "Share link with 5 friends") to earn **XP**.
*   **Get AI Advice:** Stuck? Click this button. Our Gemini AI analyzes your real-time sales data and gives you a personalized strategy tip in seconds.

## 2. Marketplace: How to Earn

Navigate to the **Marketplace** tab to see the products available for distribution.

### Browsing & Details
*   **View Details:** Click on any product card to open the **Product Detail Page**. Here you can see high-resolution images, detailed descriptions, and your exact commission calculation.
*   **Inventory:** Stock levels are real-time. If a product says "Low Stock", act fast!

### How to Sell
1.  **Copy Link:** Click the "Share" or "Copy Affiliate Link" button.
2.  **Send:** Paste this link to your customers via Zalo, Facebook, or TikTok.
3.  **Earn:** When they buy, you instantly receive **20-25% Commission**.

### 🛒 Simulating a Sale (MVP Feature)
*For this Seed version, you can test the system yourself:*
1.  Click **"Buy Now"** on a product card or detail page.
2.  Watch for the success confirmation ("Added to Wallet").
3.  Go to your **Dashboard** or **Wallet** immediately—you will see your *Personal Sales* and *Earnings* update instantly!

> **Tip:** If a product says "Out of Stock", the Buy button will be disabled.

## 3. My Wallet: Getting Paid & Taxes

We believe in **Radical Transparency**. Go to the **My Wallet** tab to manage your income.

### 💳 The "ATM" Card
*   **Withdrawable Balance:** The actual money you can take home right now.
*   **Total Earnings:** The total revenue you generated before tax.

### 🏛️ Auto-Tax Compliance (Vietnam Law)
WellNexus handles legal compliance for you automatically.
*   **The Rule:** According to Vietnamese law (Circular 111/2013/TT-BTC), any income payment over **2,000,000 VNĐ** is subject to a **10% Personal Income Tax (PIT)** deduction.
*   **How it looks:**
    *   If you earn **375,000 đ**, you receive **375,000 đ** (No tax).
    *   If you earn **5,000,000 đ**, we withhold **500,000 đ** (10%) and you receive **4,500,000 đ**.
*   **Receipts:** Scroll down to "Earnings History" to see exactly how much tax was deducted for every single transaction.

## 4. Quick Troubleshooting

**Q: Why is my "Buy Now" button disabled?**
A: The product is likely **Out of Stock**. Check the stock counter on the card.

**Q: My AI Advice isn't loading.**
A: The AI "thinks" for about 1-2 seconds. If it takes longer, check your internet connection.

**Q: How do I level up my Rank?**
A: Focus on **Team Volume**. As your downline sells products, your Team Volume grows, moving you closer to the "Partner" and "Founder" ranks.

**Q: I made a sale but don't see it?**
A: Refresh the page. In this MVP version, simulated sales should appear instantly, but network lag can occur.
