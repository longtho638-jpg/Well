## 1. DIEU HANH CHIEN LUOC

### 1.1 Quy trinh ra quyet dinh

| Cap do | Loai quyet dinh | Ai phe duyet | Thoi gian |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|-----------|
| L1 | Van hanh daily (don hang, partner) | Founder/Admin | Ngay |
| L2 | Thay doi san pham, gia, commission | CEO | 1-3 ngay |
| L3 | Thay doi cong nghe, doi tac moi | CEO + CTO | 1 tuan |
| L4 | Thay doi mo hinh kinh doanh | CEO + Board | 2-4 tuan |

### 1.2 Meeting cadence

| Meeting | Tan suat | Nguoi tham gia | Output |
|---------|----------|----------------|--------|
| Daily standup | Hang ngay 9:00 | CEO + Core team | Status update |
| Weekly review | Thu 2, 14:00 | CEO + All leads | KPI dashboard |
| Monthly strategy | Ngay 1, 10:00 | CEO + Founders | Strategy doc |
| Quarterly board | Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4 | CEO + Investors | Board deck |

### 1.3 OKR Framework

| Quy | Muc tieu (O) | Ket qua then chot (KR) |
|-----|-------------|------------------------|
| Q1 | Launch & Traction | KR1: 100 partners, KR2: 50M GMV, KR3: 80% retention |
| Q2 | Growth | KR1: 500 partners, KR2: 200M GMV, KR3: Unit economics + |
| Q3 | Scale | KR1: 2000 partners, KR2: 1B GMV, KR3: 3 tinh moi |
| Q4 | Profitability | KR1: EBITDA duong, KR2: 5000 partners, KR3: Series A ready |

## 2. TAI CHINH & P&L

### 2.1 Co cau doanh thu

| Nguon | Mo ta | Ti le muc tieu |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| Product margin | Chenh lech gia nhap vs gia ban | 40-50% |
| Commission fee | Platform fee tren moi giao dich | 5-10% |
| Subscription | Goi premium cho partner | 10-15% |
| AI services | Copilot, Health Coach, Analytics | 5-10% |

### 2.2 Co cau chi phi

| Hang muc | Nguong % doanh thu | Ghi chu |
|----------|-------------------|---------|
| COGS (Gia von hang ban) | < 50% | Thuong luong nha cung cap |
| Commission payout | < 25% | Bee 2.0: 21-25% theo rank |
| Tech infrastructure | < 10% | Supabase, Vercel, Sentry |
| Marketing | < 15% | CAC phai < LTV/3 |
| Nhan su | < 20% | Core team 5-8 nguoi giai doan dau |
| Legal & compliance | < 5% | Giay phep, ke toan, thue |

### 2.3 Bao cao tai chinh

| Bao cao | Tan suat | Nguoi lam | Gui cho |
|---------|----------|-----------|---------|
| Cash flow statement | Hang tuan | CFO/Ke toan | CEO |
| P&L statement | Hang thang | CFO/Ke toan | CEO + Board |
| Balance sheet | Hang quy | Ke toan | CEO + Investors |
| Tax filing | Hang quy | Ke toan | Co quan thue |
| Runway projection | Hang thang | CEO | Internal |

### 2.4 Runway management

| Metric | Canh bao | Hanh dong |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Runway < 12 thang | Vang | Bat dau fundraising |
| Runway < 6 thang | Do | Cat giam chi phi, gap investor |
| Runway < 3 thang | Khan cap | Freeze hiring, pivot hoac bridge loan |
| Burn rate tang > 20%/thang | Canh bao | Review chi phi ngay |

## 3. NHAN SU & TO CHUC

### 3.1 So do to chuc (giai doan dau)

```
CEO
├── CTO (Ky thuat)
│   ├── Frontend Dev
│   └── Backend Dev
├── COO (Van hanh)
│   ├── Partner Manager
│   └── Customer Support
├── CMO (Marketing)
│   └── Content Creator
└── CFO (Tai chinh, part-time)
```

### 3.2 Quy trinh tuyen dung

| Buoc | Hanh dong | Thoi gian |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | Xac dinh nhu cau (JD) | 1 ngay |

| 3 | Loc CV | 3-5 ngay |

| 5 | Phong van vong 2 (Technical/Role) | 60 phut |
| 6 | Offer | 1 ngay |

| Vai tro | Range (VND/thang) | Equity |
|---------|-------------------|--------|

| Senior Dev | 20-30M | 1-2% |
| Junior Dev | 10-15M | 0.5-1% |

| Marketing | 10-20M | 0.5-1% |

### 3.4 Van hoa & gia tri cot loi

| Gia tri | Mo ta |
|---------|-------|

## 6. GOI VON & INVESTOR

### 6.1 Giai doan goi von

| Vong | So tien | Dinh gia | Muc dich |
|------|---------|----------|----------|
| Pre-seed | 500M-1B VND | 5-10B | MVP + 100 partners |
| Seed | 2-5B VND | 20-50B | Growth + 1000 partners |
| Series A | 10-20B VND | 100-200B | Scale + nhieu tinh |

### 6.2 Tai lieu cho investor

| Tai lieu | Muc dich | Cap nhat |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Pitch deck | Trinh bay vision + traction | Hang thang |
| Financial model | Du bao 3-5 nam | Hang quy |
| Data room | Due diligence docs | Khi can |
| Product demo | Live demo platform | Luon san sang |

### 6.3 KPI investor quan tam

| KPI | Mo ta | Muc tieu |
|-----|-------|----------|
| MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) | Doanh thu hang thang | Tang 15-20%/thang |
| GMV | Tong gia tri giao dich | Tang 20%/thang |
| CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) | Chi phi co 1 partner | < LTV/3 |
| LTV (Lifetime Value) | Gia tri tron doi partner | > 3x CAC |
| Churn rate | Ti le partner roi mang | < 5%/thang |
| NPS (Net Promoter Score) | Do hai long | > 50 |

## 7. MO RONG & SCALING

### 7.1 Roadmap mo rong dia ly

| Giai doan | Khu vuc | Dieu kien |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Now | TP.HCM | Pilot + product-market fit |
| Q2 2026 | Ha Noi + Da Nang | 500 partners, unit economics + |
| Q3 2026 | Mien Tay + Mien Trung | 1000 partners, ops team |
| Q4 2026 | Toan quoc | 2000 partners, auto-ops |
| 2027 | ASEAN (Thailand, Indonesia) | Product-market fit VN confirmed |

### 7.2 Scaling checklist

| Hang muc | Dieu kien scale | Hien tai |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| Tech | Auto-deploy, 99.5% uptime, < 2s load | ✅ |
| Ops | SOPs cho moi flow, AI automation | ✅ |
| Finance | Unit economics duong, runway > 12 thang | ⚠️ |
| Team | Core team du nang luc | ⚠️ |
| Legal | GPKD + TMDT license | ⚠️ |
| Product | PMF confirmed (retention > 60%) | ⚠️ |

### 7.3 Rui ro & giam thieu

| Rui ro | Muc do | Giam thieu |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Canh tranh | Cao | Di nhanh, xay moat (network effect) |
| Phap ly MLM | Trung binh | Tuan thu 100%, tu van luat su |
| Cash flow | Cao | Runway management, goi von som |
| Key person risk | Trung binh | Cross-training, document moi thu |
| Tech failure | Thap | CI/CD, monitoring, DR plan |
| Market risk | Trung binh | Validate PMF truoc khi scale |
