## 🤔 Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch plans later?

**Yes!** Upgrade or downgrade anytime from Dashboard → Subscription. Changes take effect immediately.

### What happens if I exceed my limits?

- **AI/API calls:** Overage charges apply (Pro/Enterprise) or blocked (Free)
- **Storage:** Warning at 80%, blocked at 100%
- **Email:** Blocked until next cycle or upgrade

### Do you offer a free trial?

- **Pro Plan:** 14-day free trial (no credit card required)
- **Enterprise:** 30-day free trial (sales call required)

### Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime from Dashboard → Subscription. No cancellation fees.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB), bank transfer, and e-wallets (MoMo, ZaloPay, VNPay).

### Is my data secure?

Yes! We use Supabase with Row-Level Security (RLS), SSL encryption, and regular security audits.

### Do you offer refunds?

30-day money-back guarantee for Pro and Enterprise plans. No refunds for Free plan (it's free!).
