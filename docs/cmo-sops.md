## 6. PAID ADS

### 6.1 Kenh quang cao

| Kenh | Budget | Muc tieu | Doi tuong |
|------|--------|----------|-----------|
| Facebook Ads | 40% budget | Reach + Conversion | 25-45, nu, suc khoe |
| Google Ads | 30% budget | Search intent | Keywords san pham |
| TikTok Ads | 20% budget | Awareness | 18-35, viral |
| Zalo Ads | 10% budget | Retargeting | Da tuong tac |

### 6.2 Campaign workflow

| Buoc | Hanh dong |
|------|-----------|
| 1 | Xac dinh muc tieu (awareness/traffic/conversion) |
| 2 | Chon doi tuong (demographics, interests, custom audience) |
| 3 | Tao creative (hinh/video + copy) |
| 4 | Setup campaign + budget |
| 5 | A/B test (2-3 variant) |
| 6 | Monitor 3 ngay dau |
| 7 | Optimize: tat ad kem, tang budget ad tot |
| 8 | Scale winning ads |

### 6.3 Budget management

| Metric | Nguong | Hanh dong |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| CPC > 5,000 VND | Canh bao | Review targeting + creative |
| CTR < 1% | Kem | Doi creative |
| ROAS < 2x | Lo | Pause, review strategy |
| ROAS > 4x | Tot | Tang budget 20% |
| CAC > LTV/3 | Nguy hiem | Stop, pivot strategy |
