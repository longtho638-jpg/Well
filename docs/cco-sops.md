## 3. QUAN LY PIPELINE

### 3.1 Sales pipeline stages

```
Lead → Qualified → Demo/Trial → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost
```

### 3.2 B2C pipeline (partner-assisted)

| Stage | Hanh dong | SLA |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| Awareness | KH biet den WellNexus (ads, referral) | - |
| Interest | KH xem san pham, hoi thong tin | < 1h response |
| Consideration | KH so sanh, can tu van | Partner follow up |
| Purchase | KH dat hang | < 24h xu ly |
| Retention | KH mua lai | 30 ngay email series |

### 3.3 B2B pipeline

| Stage | Hanh dong | SLA |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| Lead gen | Tim doanh nghiep tiem nang | Lien tuc |
| Qualification | Xac dinh nhu cau, budget | < 48h |
| Meeting | Trinh bay giai phap | 1 tuan |
| Proposal | Gui bao gia + dieu khoan | 3 ngay |
| Negotiation | Dam phan gia, so luong | 1-2 tuan |
| Closing | Ky hop dong, setup | 1 tuan |
| Onboarding | Cai dat, dao tao | 2 tuan |

## 5. PRICING & PACKAGING

### 5.1 Pricing strategy

| Strategy | Mo ta |
|----------|-------|
| Cost-plus | COGS + margin 40-60% |
| Competitive | So sanh doi thu ±10% |
| Value-based | Theo gia tri cam nhan |
| Bundle | Combo giam gia 10-20% |

### 5.2 Price tiers

| Tier | Doi tuong | Giam gia |

| Partner | Doi tac ban hang | 21-25% commission |

| VIP | KH than thiet | 10% off + free ship |

## 7. REVENUE OPERATIONS

| Metric | Cong cu | Tan suat |

| Phuong phap | Dung khi |

| Lever | Hanh dong | Impact |
