## 2. NGAN SACH & BUDGET

### 2.1 Budget allocation (% doanh thu)

| Hang muc | % Target | Ghi chu |
|----------|---------|--------|
| COGS | < 50% | Dam phan NCC |
| Commission | < 25% | Bee 2.0 rates |
| Marketing | < 15% | CAC < LTV/3 |
| Tech/Infra | < 10% | Supabase, Vercel, AI |
| HR/Salaries | < 20% | Core team |
| Legal/Admin | < 5% | Giay phep, ke toan |
| Reserve | > 5% | Emergency fund |

### 2.2 Budget approval process

| So tien | Ai duyet | Thoi gian |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| < 5M VND | Department head | Ngay |
| 5-20M VND | CFO | 1-2 ngay |
| 20-50M VND | CFO + CEO | 3 ngay |
| > 50M VND | CEO + Board | 1 tuan |

### 2.3 Cost control measures

| Hang muc | Bien phap |
|----------|----------|
| Tech | Dung free tiers, optimize usage |
| Marketing | Track ROAS, cut underperforming ads |
| HR | Lean team, contractors for non-core |
| Office | Remote-first, co-working |
| Travel | Virtual meetings uu tien |

## 3. CASH FLOW MANAGEMENT

### 3.1 Cash flow monitoring

| Metric | Tan suat | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Daily cash position | Hang ngay | Internet banking |
| Weekly cash forecast | Hang tuan | Spreadsheet |
| Monthly cash flow statement | Hang thang | Accounting SW |
| Runway calculation | Hang thang | Burn rate / Cash |

### 3.2 Cash flow optimization

| Strategy | Mo ta |
|----------|-------|
| Collect fast | PayOS instant settlement |
| Pay slow (ethically) | NCC Net 30 terms |
| Reduce inventory | Just-in-time ordering |
| Minimize receivables | Prepayment for B2C |
| Buffer | Giu 3 thang chi phi |

### 3.3 Runway alerts

| Runway | Alert | Action |
|--------|-------|--------|
| > 12 thang | Xanh | Invest in growth |
| 6-12 thang | Vang | Start fundraising |
| 3-6 thang | Do | Cut costs, bridge loan |
| < 3 thang | Khan cap | Freeze all, pivot |

## 5. BAO CAO TAI CHINH

### 5.1 Bao cao dinh ky

| Bao cao | Tan suat | Nguoi nhan | Noi dung |
|---------|----------|------------|----------|
| P&L Summary | Hang tuan | CEO | Revenue, costs, margin |
| Cash Flow | Hang thang | CEO + Board | In/out/net, runway |
| Balance Sheet | Hang quy | CEO + Investors | Assets, liabilities |
| Budget vs Actual | Hang thang | All leads | Variance analysis |
| Tax Report | Hang quy | CEO | Tax obligations |
| Investor Update | Hang thang | Investors | KPIs, financials |

### 5.2 P&L template

```
=== P&L THANG [MM/YYYY] ===
Revenue (Doanh thu)          [amount]
  - B2C Sales                [amount]
  - Partner Sales             [amount]
  - B2B Sales                 [amount]
(-) COGS (Gia von)           [amount]
= Gross Profit (Loi nhuan gop) [amount]  [margin%]

(-) Operating Expenses
  - Commission Payout         [amount]
  - Marketing                 [amount]
  - Tech/Infrastructure       [amount]
  - Salaries                  [amount]
  - Admin/Legal               [amount]
= EBITDA                      [amount]  [margin%]

(-) Other (Depreciation, Tax)  [amount]
= Net Income                   [amount]  [margin%]
```

## 6. COMMISSION & PAYOUT

### 6.1 Commission accounting

| Buoc | Hanh dong |
|------|-----------|
| 1 | Don hang completed → commission tinh tu dong |
| 2 | Ghi nhan Commission Payable (no phai tra) |
| 3 | Partner yeu cau rut tien |

### 6.2 Payout schedule

| Tan suat | Dieu kien | Phuong thuc |
|----------|-----------|-------------|

|---------|----------|
| Tong commission vs doanh thu | Hang tuan |
